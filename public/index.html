HOW TO PLAY — keep-up keep-away
Keep the ball in the air by timing touches at the right stance: feet kick-up, knee pop, head nod. The camera follows the ball.
Tap Space for a gentle juggle; hold it to charge a bigger launch. Out-time your opponent's touch to steal it. A dead ball on the ground only comes back to life with a feet kick-up. Punt it past them into the goal.
